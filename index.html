Objectives of Receiving Fulbright ETA Assistance

SMA Xaverius seeks Fulbright ETA support for the 2024/2025 academic year to enhance English language learning by improving students' speaking skills and understanding of American culture. The presence of ETAs will enrich the educational experience and reinforce the school's commitment to providing high-quality, globally-oriented education.

Proposed Activities with ETA in the Next 8 Months

  • Increasing student interaction with ETA to enhance communication skills.
  • Developing extracurricular programs to improve English proficiency.
  • Organizing a weekly English Day.
  • Implementing the Pancasila Student Character Strengthening Project.
  • Creating Community Engagement Projects (CEP).
